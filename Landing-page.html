About Our District

Balasore is a vibrant community known for its rich history and modern amenities. With scenic parks, cultural landmarks, and a welcoming atmosphere, it's a great place to live and visit.

Balasore also known as Balasore is a coastal district of Odisha located on the northernmost part of the state. It is believed to be a part of Kalinga that later on became a territory of Toshal or Utkal.

Local Attractions

Temples

Panchalingeswar Temple tourist places in Balasore is a sacred Hindu shrine. The temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva.

The Emami Jagannath Temple, Kshirachora Gopinatha Temple, and Baba Bhusandeswar Temple is the largest Shiva Linga in Asia. The 12 feet long and 14 feet width lingam is carved out on black granite situated in Balasore.

Seabeach

Dublagadi Sea Beach is a virgin beach in Orissa and is an off-beat destination where you can experience the magical phenomenon of Blue Tide, caused by luminescent marine life.

Chandipur is a popular beach town situated in the Balasore District of Orissa. It lies by the Bay of Bengal. The distance is 16 km from Balasore Railway Station.

City of Attraction

The Indian Ballistic Missile Defense Program's Integrated Test Range is located 18 km south of Balasore. The Defence Research and Development Organisation developed many different missile systems.
